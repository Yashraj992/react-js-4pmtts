What is video tag in HTML?

  • With use of video tag, we can embed video in webpage.
  • There are many attributes of <video> tag.
  • autoplay:- When the page is loaded video will be play as soon as possible.
  • controls:- It displays video control.
  • loop:- video start again and again.
  • muted:- When the page is loaded video will be automatically mute.
  • src:- URL of the video file.
  • To embed video in HTML, we can use the <video> tag.
